About Us

Scoots is a scooter rental company specializing in renting high-end scooters to tourists. We are based in Whidbey Island, WA. If you happen to be in the Whidbey Island area and are in need of gas-efficient form of transportation, stop by!

We carry some of the neatest scooters on the market, decked out with best paint jobs and chrome rims! But if that's not your style, we also have plenty of old school style scooters to choose from.

Our prices will fit any budget. Not only will you save money on gas, but you'll also save money on the rental itself. Why rent an expensive car when you can get an awesome scooter for much less of your dough?

As a wise man once said:

"Dough comes and dough goes. Don't waste any of it."
