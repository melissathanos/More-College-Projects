The Final Project

This website was created to be the final project for CITPT 111. It includes information on a company that is located in Seattle, Washington. The logo is a creation of the instructor's and is the official logo used for Inland Empire Imports. This website incorporates a few jQuery modules or scripts. It has an expandable menu, horizontal menu layout, and a stripy table.

Inland Empire Imports

This company is located in Seattle, Washington and is a major importer of exotic fruits and other foods from South America. Our main products are fruits, but we also import some of the best coffee South America has to offer.

Our Products

We import the best coffee in the United States from Columbia, South America. We also import exotic fruits like the ugli fruit, papaya, and mangoes. You can contact us through the contact page if there are products you would like to see in your local market.
