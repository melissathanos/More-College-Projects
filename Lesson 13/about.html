Our Mission

Our company strives to provide the best products and services in the industry. The company provides top notch customer service and will strive to keep every customer satisfied. We know that the customer is the most important asset to our business and service. We believe in providing honest work for an honest wage and a product that is shipped with the utmost care. Our mission reflects how we wish to be treated as our customers, being the most important asset, will be treated in the same manner.

Company Mission

Inland Empire Imports has the mission of providing customer service at a professional level. Our mission includes providing a product that meets the customer's needs and specifications. Our products are shipped with the utmost care and professionalism. Our customers, being our most important asset, will receive a great product at a great price and the best customer service we are able to offer. Great products, the best prices, and top notch customer service with professional results are what our mission is all about.

The customer is our most valued asset. With this in mind the service provided to our customers and their families will be of a professional quality and nature. Problems with any of our products will be remedied as soon as it is possible, when the customer contacts us in the time allowed as per our return policy. Customer data will be kept safe and confidential as per our privacy policy. In short, Rainbow Notions will provide the best price, a great product, and professional customer service.
